PowerPoint | Excel


Since I got my first laptop in 2003 I've always enjoyed experimenting with interactive content. Over the years I discovered ways of pushing the limits of Microsoft Office to do things that exceed expectation...

Beyond the typical use cases of presentations and spreadsheets, here I've included a selection of games to showcase my skills and demonstrate the capabilities of these seemingly mundane everyday applications.

It goes without saying that you should always be weary when downloading files. Visual Basic for Applications is particularly easy for malicious users to exploit and you should only ever run macros from trusted sources. The macros used in the Excel games hosted here are safe - however please think twice before opening copies found elsewhere on the internet.
